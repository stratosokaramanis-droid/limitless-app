## The Idea

Every day is an episode of your show. You're not "doing routines" — you're playing a game across two dimensions: the outer game (sleep, nutrition, dopamine, mood) and the inner game (conviction, resistance, key decisions, presence). The system tracks both, connects them, and documents the journey.

## Full Day Flow

### 🌅 Wake Up
1. **Open Faith** — she greets you, gets you present. Logs your sleep data and morning state (energy, clarity, mood).
2. **Send screenshots to Ruby** — Sleep Cycle screenshot (sleep score) + FitMind screenshot (meditation score). She extracts the numbers.
3. **Open the app → Flow tab** — Start your morning routine cards. Each item is hold-to-complete.
4. **Dawn check-in** (optional) — if you want a coached morning conversation.

### 🎨 Creative Block (3 hours)
1. Do your creative work.
2. **When done → hit Muse** — she debriefs. What'd you work on? How's your energy? What'd you eat? She logs nutrition, dopamine quality, creative output, insights.

### ⚡ Work Sessions (3 × 90 min)
1. **Message Forge** to start a session — he logs focus area and evaluation criteria.
2. Work for 90 minutes.
3. **Message Forge** to end — outcomes, flow score, composite score. Meal if you ate.
4. Repeat up to 3 sessions.

### 🌞 Throughout the Day
1. **Message Ruby** whenever — "just had a protein bowl" (logs meal + nutrition score), "caught myself about to scroll and stopped" (logs key decision: resist, 3x), "going for a walk without phone" (starts dopamine farming).
2. Key decisions are THE core mechanic. Every time you choose hard-right over easy-wrong, tell Ruby. Types:
   - **resist** (3x) — resisted an urge
   - **persist** (2x) — kept going when you wanted to stop
   - **reframe** (2x) — caught a negative loop
   - **ground** (2x) — breathed through discomfort
   - **face-boss** (5x) — confronted a resistance pattern head-on
   - **recenter** (2x) — checked in, called energy back
3. **Dopamine tracking** — either through Ruby or the Dopamine tab:
   - **Farming:** deliberate unstimulated time. Walk without music, eat without screens. Timer in the app or tell Ruby. Points scale with duration (5min=1pt, 15min=3, 30min=7, 60min=15).
   - **Overstimulation:** quick-log sugar, alcohol, social media binges, gaming, streaming, excessive caffeine. No judgment, just data.

### 🌙 Night Routine (App → Flow tab)
4 phases, each with hold-to-complete cards:

**Phase 1 — Wind Down:**
- Letting Go meditation
- Nervous system regulation
- Body scan (optional)

**Phase 2 — Reflection:**
- Alter Memories — review today's negative votes, acknowledge/reframe
- Day Review — what went well, what didn't

**Phase 3 — Planning:**
- Plan Tomorrow — text input for priorities, time blocks, what to protect
- Finalize Plan — confirm or photo

**Phase 4 — Bed Routine:**
- Read Prompts — daily prompt questions
- VF Game — opens Void for tonight's exploration
- Visualization — see tomorrow clearly
- Lights Out — day is done, timestamps the end

### 🎮 VF Game (via Void bot, nightly)
The core inner game. 12 affirmations across 6 categories:
- For each affirmation: rate your **resistance** (how much pushback you feel) and **conviction** (how much you believe it)
- High resistance + high conviction = the most interesting work
- Low resistance + low conviction = numb/disconnected (its own signal)
- Log boss encounters when you face psychological resistance patterns
- Void writes a **chapter** after each session — narrative documentation of the journey

### 📺 Episode (auto-managed)
- Episode number auto-increments
- You set the title and arc ("The one where I face the comfort boss")
- Plot points auto-populate from key decisions and boss encounters
- Rate the episode at end of day
- Archive builds over time — your documented journey

## The Scores

### VF Score (0-10) — Inner Game
Composed from:
- Conviction average (25%)
- Resistance inverted (25%) — lower resistance = higher score
- Key decisions total weight (20%)
- Boss encounters faced (15%)
- Presence score (15%)

### State Score (0-10) — Outer Game
Composed from:
- Sleep (30%) — hours + quality
- Dopamine (25%) — farming points vs overstimulation vs screen time
- Mood (25%) — energy, emotional state
- Nutrition (20%) — meal scores through the day

### Mental Rank — Skill Game
7 disciplines, each with XP and tiers:
1. Reality Distortion Field
2. Frame Control
3. Fearlessness
4. Aggression (channeled intensity)
5. Carefreeness
6. Presence
7. Bias to Action

Each has exercises, missions, and boss encounters that award XP. Tiers: Initiate → Apprentice → Warrior → Champion → Master.

## Vote System

Everything generates votes. Votes are the atomic unit of the system.

| Source | Category | Polarity | Weight |
|--------|----------|----------|--------|
| Clean meal | nutrition | positive | 1 |
| Junk meal | nutrition | negative | 1 |
| Key decision (resist) | mental-power | positive | 3 |
| Key decision (face-boss) | mental-power | positive | 5 |
| Dopamine farming | mental-power | positive | pts/5 |
| Overstimulation event | mental-power | negative | 1 |
| High conviction (≥8) | mental-power | positive | 2 |
| Low conviction (≤3) | mental-power | negative | 1 |
| High resistance (≥8) | mental-power | negative | 1 |
| Night routine completion | discipline | positive | 1 |
| Badge exercise | varies | positive | varies |

Votes feed into the State tab, Home screen, and history. They're the objective record of your day.

## Philosophy

This isn't a productivity system. It's a consciousness game.

- **Votes not streaks** — every micro-decision counts, no streaks to break
- **Key decisions ARE the training** — the moment of choosing is where growth happens
- **Resistance is the signal** — high resistance means you found the boss
- **Document the journey** — episodes and chapters create narrative momentum
- **No judgment** — overstimulation gets logged, not punished. Data, not shame.
- **The fire** — the system protects the fire when the fire can't protect itself
